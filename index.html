- HELP DESK -

1. Roll Dice Button will roll the dice and whatever number pops up will be stored in your 'current' value.

2. Pressing Hold Button will store the total 'Current' value in your Total Score.

3. If the value of dice is 1, the current value becomes 0 and the next player gets chance.

4. Both players will get 10 chances. Whoever has the higest score by the end of the game will be the winner.

5. It's all about luck.
